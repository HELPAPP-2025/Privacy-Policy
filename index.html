Last Updated: 10th December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to H.E.L.P. (the “App”). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using our App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information Provided by You

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, gender, profile picture.
  • Emergency Contacts: Contact names and phone numbers imported by users.

Sensitive Information

  • Audio Recordings: Recordings made through the App for documentation purposes.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates are captured only when users trigger emergency features (Shake feature, Help button, or Inform Contacts). Location is used temporarily to share with emergency services and selected emergency contacts and is not stored permanently.

Third-Party Integrations

Groq AI: For AI-based guidance (e.g., “Do’s” and “Don’ts” during police interactions).

Future Subscription Note

H.E.L.P. may offer subscription-based features in the future. When subscriptions are available, payment information will be processed securely through Google Play’s billing system or other authorized payment processors. Users will have the option to manage or cancel subscriptions at any time.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide App functionality, including emergency contact management, AI guidance, and recording features.
  • Share location with emergency services and emergency contacts during user-initiated emergency situations.
  • Enhance user experience and improve App features.
  • Ensure compliance with applicable legal obligations.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Audio Recordings and Personal Data: Retained as long as the account remains active. Users can request account deletion directly through the App settings.
  • Emergency Contacts: Stored locally on user devices only. Retained for the duration of account use and deleted when you uninstall the app or delete your account.
  • Location Data: Not stored in our database. Location is only captured temporarily during emergency situations and is not retained after the feature is used.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account at any time through the App settings. Upon account deletion, all personal data, including audio recordings and emergency contacts, will be permanently erased from our systems within 24-48 hours, except where retention is required by law.
