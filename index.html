Introduction

Welcome to H.E.L.P. (the “App”). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using our App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information Provided by You

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, gender, profile picture.
  • Emergency Contacts: Contact names and phone numbers added by users.

Sensitive Information

  • Audio Recordings: Recordings made through the App for documentation purposes.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates are captured only when users trigger emergency features (Shake feature, Help button, or Inform Contacts). Location is used temporarily to share with emergency services and selected emergency contacts and is not stored permanently.
